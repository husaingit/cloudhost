Can I cancel any time?

You can cancel your plan at any time with a single click in your account.
If you cancel your plan, you will have the access to cloud host theme/extensions until the day your next plan payment was due to be billed. For example, if you cancel your annual plan 4 months before your payment is due, you will still get access for another 4 months.
After your plan has expired you will still be able to work with the projects created before expiration.

What happens to my sites if I don’t renew?

Your existing sites will remain completely functional in your account, even if your 1-year plan is expired. However you won't be able to create new sites with premium themes and will lose the access to new releases, updates, and support.

Can I use themes on multiple websites?

Yes, you can use themes/extensions from the kit on an unlimited number of websites, including your own or clients' websites.
